❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my habit streaks?
Open the app, go to the habit you want to reset, and tap on the streak counter. From there, you can choose to reset your current streak. Note that your historical data will be preserved.
Does FlexStreak work offline?
Yes! FlexStreak works completely offline. Your habits and progress are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data will automatically sync when you're back online.
How is my streak calculated?
FlexStreak uses a flexible 70% completion threshold. If you complete at least 70% of your habits in a week, your streak continues. This approach celebrates progress over perfection!
Can I export my data?
Absolutely! Go to Settings → Export Data to export your habits and completion history as JSON or CSV files. You can also import previously exported data.
Does FlexStreak integrate with Apple Health?
Yes! FlexStreak can sync with Apple Health to track your steps and mindful minutes. Go to Settings → Health Integration to enable this feature.
How do I delete my account and data?
We respect your data rights. To delete your data, simply uninstall the app from your device. If you need help with specific data deletion requests, please contact us at iocompile67692@gmail.com.
